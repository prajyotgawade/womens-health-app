# Release Notes - v1.0.0

**Release Date:** July 2026
**Version Code:** 1
**Package:** `com.gawad.womenshealth`

## Welcome to Women's Health v1.0! 🎉

We are thrilled to launch the definitive production version of our comprehensive health tracking platform.

### Major Features
*   **Smart Cycle Engine:** Highly accurate period and ovulation predictions powered by your personal historical data.
*   **Premium Daily Logs:** Track over 30 unique health data points including flow, mood, symptoms, and medication.
*   **Clinical Reports:** Instantly generate and export beautiful PDF analytics to share with your healthcare provider.
*   **AI Health Assistant:** 24/7 localized guidance for managing symptoms and understanding your cycle.
*   **Health Library:** A heavily categorized reading library spanning PCOS, Pregnancy, Nutrition, and more.
*   **Offline Resilience:** Native error boundaries and state caching ensure your app never crashes, even without cellular service.

### Security Enhancements
*   Strict Row Level Security (RLS) enforcement on all cloud storage.
*   Anonymous analytics toggles and instantaneous permanent account deletion capabilities.
