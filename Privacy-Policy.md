# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Women's Health. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and securing your highly sensitive health data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Data Collection
We collect the following data to provide personalized health tracking:
*   **Account Data:** Email address (via Google/Supabase Auth).
*   **Health Data:** Period logs, symptoms, moods, medications, and weight tracking.
*   **Device Data:** Crash logs and anonymous analytics (only if explicitly enabled in Settings).

## 3. Data Storage & Security
*   **Cloud Syncing:** Your data is securely encrypted in transit and at rest using PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS).
*   **Access Control:** No one, not even our engineers, can view your personalized health logs without your authenticated session token.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We **never** sell your health data to third-party advertisers. 
We only share data with strict sub-processors required to operate the app (e.g., Supabase for database hosting).

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
*   Export your complete health record via PDF at any time.
*   Permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the Settings menu.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@gawad.womenshealth.com.
